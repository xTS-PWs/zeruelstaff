Welcome to Zeruel LLC

Welcome to Zeruel LLC! At Zeruel, we pride ourselves on being a friendly and supportive grocery store that values our employees as much as our customers. This handbook outlines our policies and expectations to help you succeed in your role.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

At Zeruel LLC, our mission is to provide high-quality groceries and exceptional service to our community.

Our Values

  • Integrity in all interactions
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Respect for all individuals
  • Excellence in service

Employment Policies

Equal Opportunity Employment

Zeruel LLC is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive, positive, and safe environment for all employees.

At-Will Employment

Your employment with Zeruel LLC is at-will, meaning either you or the company can terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause.

Work Hours & Breaks

Standard Work Hours

Employees are expected to work their scheduled shifts. Any changes to the schedule should be discussed with a supervisor in advance.

Work Hour Rules

  • No Eating in Front: During business hours, eating is strictly prohibited in the front part of the store. All meals should be consumed in designated break areas only.
  • Music Policy: Only music from the approved playlist by the owner is allowed. Personal music or unapproved playlists are not permitted.
  • Work During Shifts: You are expected to be working during all scheduled hours. Breaks are the only exception.
  • Restricted Access: Under no circumstances should non-employees be allowed in the back office or restricted areas.

Lunch Breaks

All employees are entitled to a 30-minute lunch break for every 8 hours of work. Please coordinate breaks with your team to ensure proper coverage.

Code of Conduct

We expect all employees to behave professionally and treat each other with respect. This includes:

  • Using polite language
  • Collaborating with team members
  • Not creating a toxic/unsafe environment
  • Providing excellent customer service
  • Following our dress code policy

Attendance Policy

Attendance is crucial for our operations. If you are unable to report to work, please notify your supervisor as soon as possible. Frequent absences or tardiness may result in disciplinary action.

Compensation

Zeruel LLC offers monthly wages that will be paid according to your shift schedule. You will only be paid in full if it is found that you worked all of your shifts according to timeclocks. Speak with your supervisor for more details.

Safety & Health Guidelines

We prioritize the safety and health of our employees and customers. Please familiarize yourself with our safety procedures, including:

  • Proper handling of food products
  • Maintaining cleanliness in the workplace
  • Calling out if you are unwell
  • Reporting any hazards or unsafe conditions immediately

Asset Protection Policy

Zero Tolerance Policy

At Zeruel LLC, we have a zero-tolerance policy for theft. If you are found stealing from the store—whether it's money, products, or anything else—you will be immediately terminated. This policy applies to all employees, regardless of position.

We believe that trust and integrity are essential in maintaining a positive work environment.

Cash Register Policy

Register Float

The cash register will begin every shift with a float of 100,000 Leones.

End-of-Shift Record

At the end of each shift, the manager on duty will record the total amount in the register.

Shortages Policy

If the register comes up short, the employee responsible for ringing up sales will be held accountable. The difference will be deducted from their paycheck.

Zero Exceptions: This policy applies under all circumstances. It is essential that employees remain vigilant, ensure accurate transactions, and report issues immediately.
