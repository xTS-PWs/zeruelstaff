Theft & Asset Protection Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Zero Tolerance Policy

At Zeruel LLC, we have a strict zero-tolerance policy when it comes to stealing. If you are caught stealing, no matter who you are or what your role is, you will be immediately fired. This rule applies to everyone, without exception.

What Does This Include?

Stealing from the company is not just about money. It includes, but is not limited to:

  • Taking money from the store
  • Stealing products, whether they are for sale or not
  • Taking any property or items that belong to the store

Compliance & Penalties

At Zeruel LLC, we trust our employees to act with honesty and integrity. If that trust is broken, we will take immediate action. There is no room for theft in our company, and anyone caught doing so will face serious consequences, including losing their job.
