Employee Dress Code

Last Updated: March 2025

Overview

At Zeruel LLC, maintaining a professional and uniform appearance is essential for creating a welcoming and consistent environment for our customers. All team members are required to follow these dress code guidelines during their shifts.

Required Uniform

Company Shirt

  • The official orange Zeruel LLC shirt must be worn during all shifts
  • Shirts must be clean and well-maintained
  • No alterations or modifications to the uniform are permitted

Personal Belongings

For security and professionalism:

  • Store all personal items in designated lockers or storage areas
  • Personal belongings are not permitted in customer-facing areas
  • Zeruel LLC is not responsible for personal items - secure storage is your responsibility

Compliance

Adherence to this dress code policy is mandatory. Failure to comply may result in:

  • Verbal warning
  • Written warning
  • Disciplinary action

It is your responsibility to ensure you have a clean, presentable uniform for every shift.
