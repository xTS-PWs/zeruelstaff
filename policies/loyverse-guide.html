Loyverse Back Office Guide

Last Updated: March 2025

Overview

Loyverse's Back Office is a powerful tool for managing our store operations. This guide explains how to effectively use its key features for daily management tasks.

Daily Sales Reports

Check daily performance metrics:

  • Access Back Office > Reports > Sales Summary
  • View total sales, transactions, and average ticket size
  • Compare performance across different time periods
  • Monitor payment methods and refunds

Inventory Management

Keep track of stock levels:

  • Regular inventory counts through Items > Inventory
  • Set up low stock alerts
  • Track inventory movements and adjustments
  • Monitor product performance and turnover

Employee Performance

Monitor staff activity:

  • Track employee sales and transactions
  • Review shift reports and cash handling
  • Monitor voided transactions and discounts
  • Evaluate customer service metrics

Important Reminders

  • Always verify end-of-day reports match physical cash counts
  • Regularly backup data and check system settings
  • Report any discrepancies or technical issues immediately
  • Keep login credentials secure and confidential

Best Practices

  • Review reports daily for accuracy
  • Use categories and tags for better organization
  • Maintain detailed records of any adjustments
  • Train staff on proper POS usage
  • Regularly update product information

Support Resources

For additional help:

  • Contact IT support for technical issues
  • Visit Loyverse Help Center for tutorials
  • Consult store management for policy questions
